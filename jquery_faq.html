Life FAQ's:

Will rubber bands will last longer when refrigerated?
Yes, when a rubber band is placed in the fridge, it causes the polymers to relax. This keeps the band from breaking down as fast as it normally does.
How many ways can you make change for a dollar?
293, this includes change in dimes, quarters, and combinations of the two. Can you figure out all 293 combinations?.
How many ridges are there on a dime?
118, the ridges allow the coin to determine if it is real or fake. This was implemented on all coins before the 18th century. The ridges also make it harder to make counterfeit coins.
What are the tips of show laces called?
Aglets, these tips can also be made of metal. Its purpose is to help make the lace easier to hold when running through the holes of the shoes.
Is a jiffy a proper unit of time?
Yes, Ii is exactly 1/100th of a second. This is slower than a Planck which is sextillion times faster.
What is tiramisu?
Tiramisu translates to ‘take me to heaven’ in Italian. This implies that this dish is so good that it would take you to heaven. This Italian dessert is well-loved all over the world and has several alternative twists you can create.
How many stars are there?
There are fewer stars than there are trees on Earth. According to statistics, there are around 3 trillion trees on the planet and only about 400 billion stars in the Milky Way.
How many burgers does McDonald’s sell?
75 burgers are sold in McDonald’s every second. You can view how many burgers have been sold in real-time on the McDonald’s website. You can also track the number of other products that have also been sold from all over the world.
Can you hum while holding your nose?
No, did you try it?
Do your eyeballs grow?
Your eyeballs do not grow or change their size as you age. Generally, only the vertical measure changes, but only by a small amount. By the time we reach 20–21 years old, our eyes will be at their permanent state.

Arches

  • In 1929 President Herbert Hoover created Arches National Monument, then in 1971 Congress designated Arches as a National Park under the Nixon administration.
  • There are more than 2000 natural sandstone arches inside Arches National Park.
  • Landscape Arch has the longest span of any arch in North America, 306 feet (93.3 meters), and is the fourth longest in the world
  • The enormous sandstone boulder atop Balanced Rock is presumed to weigh approximately 3,577 tons!

Badlands

  • Bison, commonly known as buffalo, can be seen throughout the park.
  • Badlands lies in an ecological area known as a mixed-grass prairie.
  • The Badlands National Park area is known worldwide for its large number of fossil remains.
  • The name Badlands comes from the Lakota, who first called it "mako sica", or "land that is bad".

Joshua Tree

  • As part of the Desert Protection Bill, Joshua Tree National Monument was elevated to national park status on October 31, 1994.
  • Early explorer John Fremont described Joshua trees as "the most repulsive tree in the vegetable Kingdom".
  • Mormon pioneers thought the limbs of the Joshua trees resembled the upstretched arms of Joshua leading them to the promised land.
  • More than 550,000 acres of Joshua Tree National Park's nearly 800,000 acres are designated as wilderness.
